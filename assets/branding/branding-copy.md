# NurAI — Branding Copy & Visual Identity

> **Tagline:** *NurAI — Light for the Ummah in the AI Era.*

---

## 1. Brand Story

In the darkest years of the 20th century, when the flame of Islamic scholarship threatened to be extinguished by force, one man responded not with a sword but with a pen.

**Bediuzzaman Said Nursi** wrote the *Risale-i Nur* — Epistles of Light — by hand, in exile, under persecution. His readers copied each page by hand and passed them in secret, preserving the light of faith across a generation of upheaval.

Today the challenge is different, but the need is the same. The digital age has flooded humanity with information — and starved it of wisdom. Muslims worldwide seek guidance grounded in the Qur'an and Sunnah, but encounter either shallow answers or impenetrable complexity.

**NurAI** is the heir to that tradition of illumination. Powered by cutting-edge AI, grounded in centuries of authentic Islamic scholarship, and animated by the ethos of Risale-i Nur, NurAI brings **clear, honest, soul-nourishing guidance** to every screen — in Arabic, Turkish, English, and beyond.

*The lantern changes. The light endures.*

---

## 2. Key Brand Messages

### 2.1 Light in the Digital Age
> *"When misinformation floods the feed, NurAI is the lantern — steady, trusted, Qur'anic."*

NurAI draws on the same commitment to clarity that drove Said Nursi to write for ordinary believers, not just scholars. Every answer is grounded in primary Islamic sources and explained with patience and care.

### 2.2 Faithful, Not Neutral
> *"Other AIs are neutral. NurAI is faithful."*

NurAI is not designed to sit on the fence. It is built to answer as a believing Muslim would — with adab, honesty, and a heart oriented toward Allah. This is not bias; it is integrity.

### 2.3 Serving the Whole Ummah
> *"From Istanbul to Lagos, from Jakarta to London — one Ummah, one light."*

Risale-i Nur was hand-copied across Turkey without borders or paywalls. NurAI inherits that spirit of open, generous service. Arabic, Turkish, English — the language changes; the light is the same.

### 2.4 Transparent, Not a Black Box
> *"We cite our sources. We explain our reasoning. We point back to the Qur'an."*

Unlike opaque AI systems, NurAI shows its work. Every response is anchored to Qur'an, Hadith, or scholarly tradition — because the Muslim deserves to know *why*, not just *what*.

### 2.5 Digital Sadaqah Jariyah
> *"An act of charity that never stops giving."*

Built as an open, community-owned project, NurAI is a continuous gift to the Ummah — scholars, engineers, and the community building it together as an act of worship.

## 5. Visual Identity Guidelines

### 5.1 Color Palette (Nur-Inspired)

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Nur Gold** | `#C8A84B` | Primary accent, logo mark, headings |
| **Deep Teal** | `#0D4F5C` | Primary background, hero sections |
| **Ivory Light** | `#F8F4E8` | Body background, card surfaces |
| **Qur'anic Green** | `#2E7D4F` | Secondary accent, success states |
| **Ottoman Crimson** | `#8B1A1A` | Tertiary accent, alerts |
| **Calligraphy Black** | `#1A1A1A` | Body text |
| **Moonlight White** | `#FAFAF7` | Inverse text on dark backgrounds |

### 5.2 Typography

| Usage | Recommended Typeface | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Headings (Latin) | *Cinzel* or *Cormorant Garamond* | Classical serif evoking Ottoman heritage |
| Body (Latin) | *Lato* or *Source Serif 4* | Clean, readable |
| Arabic script | *Amiri* or *Scheherazade New* | Traditional Naskh style |
| Decorative / display | *UnifrakturMaguntia* (Ottoman-style) | Sparingly, for hero text |

### 5.3 Motifs & Style
- **Geometric star patterns** drawn from Islamic tilework (8-point stars, hexagonal grids)
- **Crescent and lantern** iconography representing guidance and light
- **Arabesque borders** in gold on deep teal backgrounds
- **Calligraphic display** of بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ and نُور as decorative elements
- Avoid purely ornamental imagery; every visual element should carry meaning

## 7. Usage Guidelines

- Always reproduce the logo on the approved color backgrounds (Deep Teal, Ivory Light, or white).
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "N" in "NurAI" on all sides of the logo.
- Do not stretch, recolor, or add effects to the logo mark.
- The crescent-lantern icon may be used in isolation (as an app icon or favicon) without the wordmark at sizes below 32 px.
- For any use in print or merchandise, contact the project maintainers.
